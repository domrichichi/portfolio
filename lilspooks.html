Spooked

This project focuses on concept development, character design, and illustration. The goal was to create over 50 uniquely designed characters and incorporate all of them into a scene. There is a heavy use of composition and negative space to ensure there is enough breathing room for each character. This also ensures that the viewer can look at each character without any visual tension.

Developing illustrated characters was taken to another level when animating them. A walk cycle is used to further express the squishy and playful tone and manner of these characters.
