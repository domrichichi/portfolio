Font Design

An ultra condensed font specifically designed for your every day satanist or horror movie enthusiast. Each poster highlights a specific feature of the font that supports its haunting, yet beautiful aesthetic.

Inspired by early renaissance sculpture, Domonic’s ultra condensed shape gives it a beautiful, yet haunting feel. With this condensed yet ornate form, each glyph can be used as its own single piece of art, or can be used as a powerful centrepiece for any form of print-media digital media.

As one of the most obvious features of Domonic, curved finials are what makes the font seem sleek but eerie at the same time. The finials are made throughout the font using the same sized circle which keeps a sense of harmony throughout all of the glyphs.

Much like tombstones in a graveyard, Domonic’s letterforms have a strong use of figure ground relationship. The glyphs finials and strokes end with sharp slices that conform to the white space around other rounded characters. The letter structures, along with the negative space around them, create a sense of unity and connectedness.
