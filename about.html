Hey! I'm Dom

I'm a graphic designer in Ottawa, Canada Branding, Motion Design, and Illustration

My background includes deep interests in visual art, which lead me to pursue a degree in art history and theory, where I cultivated my understanding and capacities for the dramatic, the decorative, the innovative and the memorable.

My design work is reflective of my enthusiastic attitude, my passion for helping others, and my love of collaboration. I'm always trying to learn new things and am always looking for inspiration.

Dom provided graphic design support to one of Causeway's self-employment clients and was also instrumental in the branding of one of our new programs. Within a short period of time Dom's creative talents shined, which was complimented by his high level of professionalism. Lana Hann Causeway Work Centre
